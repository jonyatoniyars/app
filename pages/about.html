About BPDA Telemedicine

Bridging Healthcare Access Through Technology

Our Mission

BPDA Telemedicine is dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare delivery through innovative digital solutions. We enable seamless collaboration between Health Workers and Doctors, ensuring quality care reaches underserved communities.

Our Vision

To create a healthcare ecosystem where distance is no barrier to quality medical consultation and care delivery, powered by technology and human compassion.

Key Features

  • Real-time Collaboration: Health Workers and Doctors collaborate on patient care in real-time
  • Digital Prescriptions: Secure, trackable prescription management with Google Docs integration
  • Video Telehealth: Integrated video consultations via Google Meet API
  • Prescription Templates: Customizable templates for General and Dental care
  • Admin Dashboard: Comprehensive analytics and user management
  • Mobile-Responsive: Access care from any device, anywhere

Platform Highlights

Multi-role Architecture: Designed for Admin, Doctor, and Health Worker personas with tailored workflows.

Secure & Compliant: Built with RBAC, audit logging, and data privacy in mind.

Scalable: Modern tech stack supporting growth across regions.
